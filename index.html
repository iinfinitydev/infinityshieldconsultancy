WHO WE ARE

House of Expertise in Fire Consultancy

Infinity Shield Fire Safety Consultancy LLC is a specialist consultancy firm providing expert guidance across the full spectrum of fire safety and compliance.

With 20 years of experience gained in the United Kingdom, we bring international best practice, rigorous technical standards, and world-class fire engineering knowledge to the UAE market.

Our consultancy services help clients design safer buildings, meet UAE Fire & Life Safety Code requirements, and ensure complete lifecycle compliance from concept through to handover.

“Expert-led. Standards-driven. Compliance assured.”
